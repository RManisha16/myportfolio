Insurance Dataset

The project concludes that the random forest classifier is more accurate than the decision tree classifier for this specific problem. The report also identifies the most important features for predicting smoker status, finding that charges is the most significant feature, with a feature importance score of 0.88, followed by bmi(0.08), children(0.015), region(0.014), and sex(0.006).
